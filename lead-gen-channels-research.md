## Section 1 — Channel inventory (the full universe)

Each channel: 1-line description · effort tier · capital · time-to-first-lead · realistic conversion · tier verdict · why it works (or doesn't) for ZentroTECH at month 0.

### A. Owned-property channels

**1. Website SEO — programmatic + content**
Programmatic landing pages (city × service) + 1 long-form pillar per week.
- Effort: **Medium** (4–6 hrs/wk to write new pillars; programmatic spine already shipped)
- Capital: ₹0 (Vercel free / next.js stack already paid for)
- Time-to-first-lead: 30–60 days for branded queries; 90–180 days for non-brand long-tail
- Realistic conversion: at current 11 visitors/24h → ~330/month → 1–3 form fills → 0–1 closed in month 1
- **Tier 1 — do now**
- Why: the 5,050-page spine is a moat *if* we differentiate from Troika Tech's per-city sprawl (which is what we're directly competing against on long-tail). The programmatic spine is the unfair-advantage asset we already own and competitors can't replicate in 6 months.

**2. Google Business Profile / Maps**
Local SEO for "WhatsApp chatbot agency Bangalore," "AI voice agency near me."
- Effort: **Low** (45 min/wk — weekly posts, photo uploads, review requests)
- Capital: ₹0
- Time-to-first-lead: 14–45 days post-verification
- Realistic conversion: 5–30 GBP views/day → 1–3 calls/wk after rank stabilises → 1 closed every 4–6 wks
- **Tier 1 — do now** (already in setup)
- Why: zero competitor research shows DigiOn, Brand Spirit Labs, Jinconnect, or Web Converts with optimised GBP profiles. This is the cheapest local-intent lead channel in Bangalore.

**3. LinkedIn personal posting (founder-led)**
Daily/3x-weekly posts from founder profile, brand voice = operator + numbered case studies.
- Effort: **Medium** (30 min/day if you have stories; covered in `linkedin-30day-playbook.md`)
- Capital: ₹0 (LinkedIn Premium ₹2,499/mo optional)
- Time-to-first-lead: 14–30 days for warm DMs from 1st-degree network; 60–90 days for stranger inbound
- Realistic conversion: 0–2 inbound DMs/wk by day 30 → 1–3 calls/mo → 1 closed in month 2
- **Tier 1 — do now**
- Why: this is the single channel where **every successful Indian SMB agency founder shows up**. Gautam Shelley (AiSensy), Aniket Bajpai (LimeChat), Vinayak Aggarwal (BiteSpeed), Ajay Singhadiya (OperateAI) — all founder-led on LinkedIn. The pattern is universal. Skip this and we lose to every competitor by default.

**4. LinkedIn cold DMs / connection outreach**
50 targeted connect requests/day with a 1-line note; follow-up DM after accept.
- Effort: **Medium** (45 min/day; the LinkedIn playbook already maps this)
- Capital: ₹0 to ₹6,000/mo (Sales Navigator ₹4,999/mo unlocks the real targeting)
- Time-to-first-lead: 7–14 days from start (the first 5–10 calls come from your *existing* 1st-degree network)
- Realistic conversion: 50 connects/day × 25% accept × 5% reply = ~3 conversations/day → 1–2 calls/wk → 1–2 closed/mo by month 2
- **Tier 1 — do now**
- Why: published pricing exists nowhere except OperateAI (₹45K–₹1.2L setup) and Jinconnect (₹19,999/mo retainer). Most Indian SMB owners have never been shown a transparent rate card. A LinkedIn DM with "we'll send our pricing if you reply" outperforms agency-generic "let's chat" by 3–5×. **This is the single highest-leverage channel for month 0–3.**

**5. LinkedIn Company Page**
Brand page; lower priority than personal posting but should mirror founder content.
- Effort: **Low** (10 min/day reposting founder content)
- Capital: ₹0
- Time-to-first-lead: 90+ days (company pages get 5–10× less organic reach than personal posts)
- Realistic conversion: ~0 in first 6 months as a direct lead source
- **Tier 2 — claim now, post weekly, but don't expect leads**
- Why: every reference competitor has one ([AiSensy](https://www.linkedin.com/company/aisensyofficial), [LimeChat](https://in.linkedin.com/company/limechat), [Jinconnect](https://in.linkedin.com/company/jinconnect), [DigiOn](https://in.linkedin.com/company/digion.in), [Web Converts](https://in.linkedin.com/company/web-converts), [Brand Spirit Labs](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/brand-spirit-labs)). It's table-stakes for verification (LinkedIn-verified org = better DM open rates) but a dry well for direct leads.

**6. X / Twitter organic**
Founder + brand accounts; daily 1–2 posts (covered in `twitter-30day-playbook.md`).
- Effort: **Low–Medium** (20 min/day)
- Capital: ₹0 (Premium ₹650/mo not worth it under 500 followers)
- Time-to-first-lead: 60–120 days; X is **terrible for direct Indian SMB inbound**
- Realistic conversion: 0–1 inbound DM/mo for first 90 days; useful for connecting with BSPs, voice-AI founders, indie devs (NOT end SMB buyers)
- **Tier 2 — month 2–3**
- Why: X is where Bolna, Ringg, Sarvam, BiteSpeed, Vapi, Retell engineers hang out. **It's a B2B2B channel for ZentroTECH (you reach partners on X, who introduce you to SMBs).** Dentists in Whitefield are not on X. Voice-AI founders signing partner programs are.

**7. Reddit organic**
r/StartUpIndia, r/IndianStartups, r/IndianEntrepreneur, niche subreddits.
- Effort: **Low** (2–4 hrs/wk per `reddit-playbook.md`)
- Capital: ₹0
- Time-to-first-lead: 60–120 days
- Realistic conversion: 0–1 lead/mo from Reddit direct; main payoff is long-tail SEO (Reddit threads rank in Google)
- **Tier 3 — month 6+, OR opportunistic if a thread directly asks "best WhatsApp agency Bangalore"**
- Why: Reddit's Indian audience is 18–30 students/junior devs; not SMB owners. Use it for SEO authority + listening post, not pipeline.

**8. Quora answering**
Answer high-intent questions: "best WhatsApp Business API India," "AI voice agent for clinic," etc.
- Effort: **Low** (30 min × 3 sessions/wk)
- Capital: ₹0
- Time-to-first-lead: 90–180 days (Quora answers compound over time; new answers buried unless they hit upvote velocity)
- Realistic conversion: 1–3 leads/mo by month 6
- **Tier 2 — month 2–3, as part of SEO authority play**
- Why: Quora threads rank on Google for "best X for Y" queries. **Brand Spirit Labs, DigiOn, Jinconnect — no evidence found** of Quora activity. Gap to fill.

### B. Outbound channels

**9. Cold email outbound**
30–80 personalised emails/day to Bangalore SMB founders.
- Effort: **High** (2–3 hrs/day list-building + sending + replies)
- Capital: ₹2,500–₹10,000/mo (Apollo ₹4,999/mo or Lemlist ₹6,500/mo; domain warmup ₹2,000/mo)
- Time-to-first-lead: 14–30 days
- Realistic conversion: 1,000 emails → 30 opens → 5 replies → 1 call → 0–1 close in month 1; **doubles by month 3** as deliverability improves
- **Tier 1 — do now (but only after LinkedIn DMs are saturated)**
- Why: works for B2B SaaS at scale (OperateAI's 23.4% reply rate case study), but Indian SMB owners *don't read email like Western buyers do*. Open rates are lower; reply rates lower. Pair every cold email with a LinkedIn DM 48 hours later.

**10. Cold WhatsApp outbound (India-specific)**
Direct WhatsApp messages to scraped owner numbers.
- Effort: **Medium** (1 hr/day for 50 manual sends)
- Capital: ₹0 (manual via personal number) to ₹3,000/mo (BSP setup)
- Time-to-first-lead: 3–14 days
- Realistic conversion: shockingly high for the first 50 sends (10–20% reply rate manual); **bans your number by message 200 if not done from BSP with consent**
- **Tier 3 — DO NOT until we have an opt-in funnel feeding it**
- Why: this is the channel everyone in India thinks they want and **shouldn't run**. Per [Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy and DPDP Act](https://www.messagecentral.com/blog/whatsapp-business-api-india-guide), unsolicited B2B WhatsApp messaging breaches platform policy. We sell WhatsApp compliance as a service — getting our own number banned is brand-suicide. (Compliance note: DLT registration is for SMS, not WhatsApp, but Meta's own policy still applies.)

**11. Phone cold-calling**
50–100 dials/day to Bangalore SMB owner numbers.
- Effort: **High** (4–5 hrs/day for the dialler + actual conversations)
- Capital: ₹0–₹3,000/mo (Exotel ₹999/mo entry)
- Time-to-first-lead: 7–14 days
- Realistic conversion: 100 dials → 25 connects → 5 conversations → 1 meeting → 0–1 close in month 1; ~₹15,000 cost per acquired client at sustainable volume
- **Tier 2 — month 2 (after LinkedIn warm pipeline is built)**
- Why: dentists and restaurant owners *answer their phones*. They don't read LinkedIn DMs. Phone outreach + a Kannada-speaking caller is genuinely differentiated in Bangalore. **Pair with the AI voice agent demo** — "Here's our voice agent. Want it answering your missed calls?" — and the call itself is the product demo.
