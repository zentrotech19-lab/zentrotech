## The single most important decision: PICK YOUR WEDGE

Before anything else this week, decide: **what specific problem do you solve for which specific buyer?** The 4 playbooks all converge on this: a brand-new consultancy with no wedge dies in 6 months from "do you do X? do you do Y?" indecision.

**Recommended wedge (from operating playbook):** *Agentic SDR systems for Indian B2B SaaS, $35-75M ARR.* Why: short sales cycle, repeatable problem, high willingness to pay, easy proof point.

**Alternatives if that doesn't fit:**
- Dubai mid-market AI automation audit ($8-15K fixed-scope sprints)
- US embedded agentic-coding pods for $5-30M dev shops
- AI-powered websites for premium professional services (legal/financial/medical)
- LLM evaluation harnesses for regulated industries

Pick ONE by end of Week 1. You can always add later.

# Week 1 — Foundation

**Goal: legal entity formed, wedge picked, tools live, first 5 LinkedIn DMs sent.**

## Day 1 (Today)
- [ ] **Pick the wedge.** Write a 2-sentence positioning statement. (90 min)
- [ ] **Engage a Bangalore CA experienced in IT exports.** Two recommended firms: ClearTax for SMB, IndiaFilings for hand-holding. Onboarding call within 48h. (60 min)
- [ ] **Apply for IEC at DGFT** — `dgft.gov.in` — ₹500, 1-3 days approval. You cannot legally bank a USD invoice without it. (30 min)
- [ ] **Apply to startup credit programs** (do all 7 today, takes 2-3h):
  - Anthropic for Startups (Claude API credits)
  - AWS Activate ($1K-$25K credits)
  - Microsoft for Startups ($25K-$150K credits)
  - Google for Startups Cloud
  - Vercel Startups (free Pro plan)
  - Notion for Startups
  - HubSpot for Startups (90% off CRM for 1 year)
- [ ] **Register zentrotech.ai** (or your final domain) at Cloudflare Registrar (~$10/yr — cheaper than Namecheap, no upselling). Point to Vercel.

## Day 2-3
- [ ] **File for Pvt Ltd incorporation** via your CA or via SPICe+ portal at MCA. Cost: ₹15-25K. Timeline: 7-10 days. Required: 2 directors (DIN + DSC), MOA, AOA, registered address (your home is fine), authorized capital ₹1L minimum.
- [ ] **Open HDFC SmartUp Current Account** (best for IT exporters; explicitly request AD-category status).
- [ ] **File LUT (Form RFD-11) on GST portal** for FY 2026-27. Saves you 18% IGST on every export invoice. (Annual filing, 30 minutes.)
- [ ] **Sign up for tools:**
  - Attio (free CRM) or HubSpot Free
  - Cal.com (free, self-hostable later)
  - Resend (transactional email — free tier)
  - Wise Business account (multi-currency, 1-3 day approval)
  - LinkedIn Sales Navigator (free 1-month trial)
  - Apollo.io or Smartlead.ai (cold email — start free)
  - 1Password Business
  - Linear (project management — free for small teams)

## Day 4-5
- [ ] **Build the 200-account ICP list.** Sales Nav filters → companies in your wedge → export to Google Sheet. Note founder/CTO/Head of Product LinkedIn URLs.
- [ ] **Rewrite LinkedIn profile** for the wedge. Headline = wedge positioning. About section = problem you solve + proof. Featured = link to ZentroTECH website + showcase page.
- [ ] **Write the discovery call playbook** (45-min agenda + 15 questions you'll ask every prospect). Save to Notion.
- [ ] **Customize MSA / SOW templates.** Get `Treelife` or `LegalRaasta` boilerplate (₹5K), then have a CA-vetted lawyer add:
  - **Publication rights clause** (THE single most important clause — without it, every successful project becomes an unpublishable case study forever). Specific text in playbook 4 Part 3.
  - IP assignment (you own delivered code unless explicitly licensed)
  - Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on milestone
  - Scope-creep handling: change order process
  - Liability cap: 12 months of fees paid
  - Governing law: Karnataka, India + DIAC Dubai arbitration option for UAE clients

## Day 6-7
- [ ] **Send first 20 LinkedIn connection requests** to people on your ICP list. Use a personalized intro (NOT "let's connect").
- [ ] **Publish your first content piece.** Either: a 1500-char LinkedIn post about your wedge OR repurpose one of the 8 blog posts already on the site as a LinkedIn carousel.
- [ ] **Buy 3 lookalike domains** for cold email (e.g., `zentrotech-mail.com`, `getzentro.io`, `tryzentro.ai`). Set them up in Apollo/Smartlead. **Start a 14-day warm-up.** You cannot send cold email from your real domain.

# Week 3 — Outbound + UAE Footprint

**Goal: cold email going live, UAE digital footprint shipped, first proposal sent.**

## Cold email
- [ ] **Domain warm-up complete (Day 21).** You can now send 100/day across the 3 lookalike domains.
- [ ] **Write the 4-touch sequence** (90-word emails, no fluff):
  - Touch 1: Specific observation about their company + question
  - Touch 2 (3 days later): Concrete idea + 1-line context
  - Touch 3 (5 days later): Case study link + 1 clear ask
  - Touch 4 (7 days later): "moving on, here's something free for you" (a relevant blog post)
- [ ] **Send 100/day across the wedge ICP.** Track: open rate (target 40%+), reply rate (target 4%+), positive reply rate (target 1%+).

## UAE digital footprint (parallel)
- [ ] Translate 1 case study + 1 services page into Arabic. Use a human translator from Fiverr/Upwork (NOT Google Translate). Cost: ~$50-100.
- [ ] Update `/ai-agency-dubai` page with translated Arabic toggle (link only — don't redesign yet).
- [ ] Activate Google Business Profile for Bangalore HQ. Verify via postcard (5-14 days). Use the 30 pre-written posts in `public/marketing/google-business-profile-content.md` as a 6-month content queue.

## Sales / pipeline

# What NOT to do in the first 30 days

- ❌ Don't open a UAE entity. Defer to Month 6+ once you have UAE clients.
- ❌ Don't hire full-time employees. Use contractors (UpWork senior engineers, fractional designers).
- ❌ Don't offer free pilots. Even $1K paid scoping is more valuable than $10K free.
- ❌ Don't pretend samples are real. Big legal + reputation risk. Label clearly.
- ❌ Don't pursue 5 wedges. Pick one. Add later.
- ❌ Don't build features. Sell, deliver, learn, sell again.
- ❌ Don't skip the publication-rights clause in MSA. Catastrophic compounding loss.
- ❌ Don't price in INR for foreign clients. Quote USD. Build the number on USD economics.
