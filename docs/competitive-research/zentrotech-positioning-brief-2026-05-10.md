## 4. The four USPs (homepage section + service-page anchors)

### USP 1 — Lead Engine Websites (not brochure sites)
- Every page funnels to a measurable conversion event
- WhatsApp + phone + form + chatbot + voice agent stitched into one funnel
- Programmatic SEO landing pages for every city + vertical we can rank for
- Outcome guarantee available on tightly-defined verticals: "no leads in 90 days = full refund"

### USP 2 — Business on Autopilot
- We map your existing manual process (sales, ops, support, follow-up, accounting)
- We replicate it as an automated workflow that runs while you sleep
- Built for solo / small-team founders who can't afford to hire an ops manager
- Every week, the autopilot handles tasks that would otherwise take 20-40 founder hours

### USP 3 — Lead + Payment Follow-Up System (flagship, India-specific)
> **"In India, you don't get paid because you didn't follow up enough times. We follow up for you."**

The follow-up automation runs in two directions:
- **Lead nurture** — every form fill / WhatsApp message / call enquiry triggers a 7-touch sequence (WhatsApp / SMS / email / scheduled call) until the lead either books a meeting or explicitly opts out. Industry data: Indian SMB lead closes after 5-7 touches; most businesses stop at 1-2.
- **Payment recovery** — every unpaid invoice triggers a polite-but-persistent reminder cadence (Day 0 / 3 / 7 / 14 / 21 / 30) via WhatsApp + email + voice call from an AI agent. Stops automatically when payment lands. Average outcome: 30-50% reduction in DSO (days sales outstanding).

This is the moat. **No competitor in our research bundles this.**

### USP 4 — No SaaS Bloat / Affordable
> **"We will never ask you to subscribe to 5 different tools. One contract with us replaces six."**

- Self-hosted n8n on a ₹2,000/mo VPS replaces Zapier (~₹6-15K/mo)
- Zoho CRM (₹1,200/user/mo) replaces HubSpot (₹7,500-12,500/user/mo)
- Bolna / Tabbly voice agents at ₹2/min replace ₹30-60/min telecaller cost
- WhatsApp Business API instead of paid chat tools
- Open-source / one-time-license stack wherever possible

The promise: **You will spend less on tools after working with us, not more.**

## 6. Pricing strategy — quote-driven, NOT published

**Public pricing is NOT shown on the website.** Decision: every project varies by scope, integrations, and ongoing needs. Publishing tier prices either anchors low (loses high-intent buyers) or anchors high (scares SMBs away on the homepage). Instead:

- Public CTA everywhere: **"Get a Custom Quote"** / **"Get Your Free Lead Engine Audit"**
- Quote process gated by a **comprehensive Requirements Form** (see section 6.1) — completing the form is mandatory before any conversation
- The form qualifies the lead AND captures expectations so the first call is high-signal

### 6.1 Requirements form — mandatory fields

The form is the actual conversion event. Designed to filter tire-kickers and front-load discovery.

**Section A — Contact (required):**
Full Name · Business Name · WhatsApp Number · Email · City (dropdown of 25+ South India cities + Other / International)

**Section B — Business (required):**
Industry / Vertical (dropdown) · Business Size (Solo / 1-5 / 6-25 / 26-100 / 100+) · Years in business · Current website (optional URL)

**Section C — What you need (multi-select, ≥1 required):**
New website · Redesign existing · Animated / 3D website · Android app · AI chatbot · AI voice agent (inbound/outbound) · Lead follow-up automation · Payment recovery automation · SEO services · CRM + lead management setup · Business workflow automation · Other (text)

**Section D — Goals (required):**
Primary goal (dropdown: More leads / Save founder time / Recover unpaid invoices / Launch online / Replace expensive subscriptions / Beat competition / Other) · Current monthly leads (range dropdown) · Investment comfort range (dropdown: Under ₹1L / ₹1-3L / ₹3-10L / ₹10L+ / Want to discuss) · Timeline (Immediately / Within 1 month / 1-3 months / 3-6 months / Just exploring)

**Section E — Description (required):**
Brief description of what you want to achieve (textarea, min 30 chars) · How did you find us? · Anything else?

**Section F — Consent (required):**
"I agree to be contacted by ZentroTECH via WhatsApp, phone, or email regarding my enquiry."

## 10. Tone of voice

- **Direct, founder-to-founder.** "We've run a Bangalore SMB. We know what eats your day. Here's how we fix it."
- **Numbers over adjectives.** "30% of leads close after the 5th touch — most agencies stop at 2." not "we're committed to follow-up excellence."
- **No marketing fluff.** "We replace 6 SaaS subscriptions with one contract" not "synergistic digital transformation."
- **India-specific honesty.** "Getting paid in India is hard without follow-up. We do the awkward part for you."
- **Outcome over feature.** "Your invoices get paid faster" not "we send automated reminders."
