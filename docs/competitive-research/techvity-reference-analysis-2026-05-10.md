**Source:** https://techvity.in (founder's existing Bangalore-based business — laptop rental / repair / sales)
**Date:** 2026-05-10
**Purpose:** Extract proven SEO + conversion patterns to apply to ZentroTECH's Bangalore pivot.

---

## Why this matters

Techvity is referenced **for SEO patterns only** — it is a separate business under the same founder, but its contact details, phone, address, email, GST, reviews, and trust numbers are NOT to be used on ZentroTECH. ZentroTECH will get its own brand identity, its own phone, its own address, its own metrics. The founder will provide all ZentroTECH-specific data separately.

## Visual language to mirror

- Sans-serif modern (Techvity feels like Inter / Geist family — already what ZentroTECH uses)
- Bold headlines, sentence case
- Lots of whitespace, clean cards
- Primary blue + neutral grays — but ZentroTECH should differentiate with **indigo + violet** (already in palette) so the two brands don't look like clones
- Multiple CTAs throughout long-scroll pages

---

## What to deliberately do *better* than Techvity

1. **Hero**: Techvity's hero is static. ZentroTECH gets a video hero (founder will provide) — instantly more premium positioning
2. **Schema markup**: Techvity is missing structured data depth (per audit). ZentroTECH ships with: Organization, LocalBusiness, Service (per service), FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Review/AggregateRating, BlogPosting (per article), VideoObject (hero video), WebSite + SearchAction
3. **Programmatic location pages**: Techvity says "250+ areas" but doesn't have 250 pages. ZentroTECH builds 15-25 actual location landing pages (`/locations/[area]`) to capture the long-tail
4. **Pricing transparency**: Techvity is mostly "request a quote." ZentroTECH publishes 3 starter package prices openly — that's a wedge competitors avoid
5. **Lead magnet**: Techvity has none. ZentroTECH offers "Free SEO + Lead Audit" as a magnet → email/WhatsApp capture
6. **Calculator widget**: "How many leads can your website get?" interactive — captures intent + email
