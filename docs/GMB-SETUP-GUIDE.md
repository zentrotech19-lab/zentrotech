## Section 5 — Verification (this is what gates everything else)

After clicking Finish, Google will offer one or more verification methods. **Pick the fastest available option in this order of preference:**

### Option A — Video verification (preferred — instant to 48h)

Most common method for Indian businesses in 2026. Google asks you to record a 1-2 minute video showing:

1. **The street outside your building** — pan to show the street sign and nearby landmarks
2. **Walk to your office entrance** — show the building name / number
3. **Your office signage** (if you have one; if not, the door / desk area is fine)
4. **Your reception or work area** — proof it's a real working office
5. **A computer/laptop on your desk showing the GMB account email** open (this proves you operate the business)

**Tips:**
- Hold phone vertically (portrait)
- Walk smoothly, no jump cuts
- Daylight is best
- Don't cut between scenes — one continuous take if possible
- Total length: 60-120 seconds

Upload via the prompt in the GMB dashboard or the GMB mobile app. **Review typically takes 1-2 business days.**

### Option B — Postcard verification (fallback — 7-14 days)

If video isn't offered or you can't do it:
1. Google mails a postcard with a 5-digit code to your office address
2. Wait 7-14 days
3. When it arrives, go back to GMB → Verify → enter the 5-digit code
4. Profile goes live

### Option C — Phone or email (rare for new businesses)

Sometimes offered for businesses with very strong online signals. If offered, take it — instant.

### What NOT to do during verification

- Don't sign in/out repeatedly
- Don't change the business name or address
- Don't add multiple locations (unless you genuinely have multiple offices)
- Don't request multiple verification methods at once
- Don't try to verify from a VPN

If verification is **rejected**: usually means your address doesn't match Google's address database, or the video didn't clearly show the business location. You'll get a chance to re-submit — review what Google flags and fix it.

## Section 9 — Get your first reviews (critical for ranking)

Reviews are the #1 local-pack ranking signal. **Aim for 10 reviews in 90 days, 25 in 180 days.**

### Step 1 — Get your direct review link

After verification:

1. Open GMB dashboard → click **"Share review form"** (or similar button)
2. Copy the direct link — it looks like: `https://g.page/r/CXxXxXxXxXxXxXxXxXxX/review`
3. **Save this link** — you'll send it to clients dozens of times

### Step 2 — First 5 reviews (ask explicitly)

Reach out to the 5 people most likely to give you a thoughtful 5-star review:

- ✅ A friend or two who knows your work and can speak to it credibly (Google rejects obviously-fake reviews from family — make sure they actually have something specific to say)
- ✅ Your first 1-3 paid clients, the moment they say "this is great" after delivery
- ✅ A prior employer or business partner who'd vouch publicly

**Script (WhatsApp):**

> Hey [name], we just launched ZentroTECH on Google Maps. If you have 60 seconds, would you mind leaving a quick honest review? Even a 1-line one helps massively. Here's the direct link: [paste link]
>
> Thanks so much 🙏

### Step 3 — Build review collection into every project

Add this to your standard project delivery flow:

- **Email signature** — "P.S. If you've worked with us recently, would you leave a Google review? Takes 30 seconds: [direct link]"
- **Post-delivery WhatsApp** — auto-message via our automation: "Project delivered ✓ — if you're happy with the work, a Google review helps other businesses find us: [direct link]"
- **Invoice footer** — link to review form

### Step 4 — Respond to EVERY review (yes, even the bad ones)

Within 24 hours of getting a review:

- **5-star:** "Thank you, [name]! It was a pleasure working with [their business]. Best of luck with the [specific outcome]!"
- **3-4 star:** "Thanks for the feedback, [name]. We hear you on [specific concern]. We'd love to make it right — DM us on +91 73489 61600?"
- **1-2 star:** Don't get defensive. "Sorry to hear that, [name]. We take feedback seriously — please WhatsApp us at +91 73489 61600 so we can understand what went wrong and fix it." Then take it offline.

Responding to reviews signals to Google that you're an active, engaged business — boosts ranking.

## Section 12 — Troubleshooting + common mistakes

| Problem | Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Verification rejected | Address mismatch with Google's database | Re-check address spelling exactly. If new address, Google may not have it indexed — request manual review via Help Center |
| Profile suspended | Google detected something suspicious (sudden category change, address mismatch, lots of edits) | Don't panic. Open GMB Help → Request Reinstatement → explain what happened. Usually reinstated in 5-7 days |
| Duplicate listing appears | Someone else (or you, accidentally) created another GMB for ZentroTECH | Report duplicate via "Suggest an edit" on the wrong one → "Place is closed or doesn't exist" |
| Wrong category showing in search | Primary category isn't optimized | Re-check Section 7.2 categories. Primary should be "Website designer" |
| Reviews not showing | Google's review filter delays new reviews 24-48h | Wait. If after 1 week still not showing, contact GMB support |
| Photos rejected | Stock photos detected or low quality | Re-upload original photos taken with your phone in daylight |
| "Temporarily closed" appears | You accidentally clicked the wrong button | Open GMB → "Mark as open" |
| Address changed without your knowledge | "Suggested edit" approved by Google from a competitor or user | Open GMB → check Activity Log → revert the change. Lock down profile via 2FA on the Google account |
