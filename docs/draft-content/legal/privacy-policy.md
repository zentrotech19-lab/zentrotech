# Privacy Policy

ZentroTECH ("ZentroTECH," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you visit our website, request information, or work with us, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

This policy is designed to align with:

- The **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** for visitors and clients in the European Union and United Kingdom
- The **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)** of India
- The **UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL)** for clients and visitors in the United Arab Emirates

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data we need. The categories below cover the routine cases.

**Information you give us directly**

- Identifiers: name, job title, company name
- Contact details: email address, phone number, country
- Project details: the message, requirements, or budget you share via our contact form, calendar booking, or email
- Engagement data: contracts, billing details, and correspondence if you become a client

**Information collected automatically**

- Device and browser information: browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- Usage information: pages viewed, referring URL, time on page, approximate location (city / country level, derived from IP)
- Cookie and similar technology data: see our [Cookie Policy](./cookie-policy.md)

**Information from third parties**

- Public profile data (e.g., LinkedIn) when you reach out and we look you up to prepare for a meeting
- Analytics aggregates from Vercel Analytics and, where enabled, Google Analytics

We do not knowingly collect special-category / sensitive personal data (race, religion, health, biometrics, sexual orientation, political opinions). Please do not send us such data through our forms.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you are, you have rights over your personal data. We honour these rights for all visitors regardless of location, to the extent operationally feasible.

**Under GDPR (EU/UK), you have the right to:**

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing)
- Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

**Under the DPDP Act (India), you have the right to:**

- Access a summary of your personal data and processing activities
- Correction, completion, updating, and erasure of personal data
- Grievance redressal
- Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity

**Under UAE PDPL, you have the right to:**

- Be informed about processing
- Access, correction, and deletion
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent

To exercise any right, email **privacy@zentrotech.com**. We will respond within **30 days** (or sooner where the law requires). We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

## 9. Security

We take security seriously and apply industry-standard safeguards:

- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all site traffic

- Periodic access reviews

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting your data, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within the timeframes the applicable law requires (e.g., 72 hours under GDPR Art. 33).

## 10. International Data Transfers

ZentroTECH operates from India and the UAE, and our vendors are largely based in the United States. This means your personal data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries outside your own — including jurisdictions that may not provide the same level of data protection as your home country.

When we transfer personal data internationally we rely on appropriate safeguards, which may include:

- **Standard Contractual Clauses** (SCCs) approved by the European Commission for transfers out of the EEA
- **UK International Data Transfer Addendum** for UK transfers
- **Adequacy decisions** where they exist
- For the UAE: a written undertaking from the recipient or other safeguards permitted under PDPL Art. 22
- For India: as permitted under the DPDP Act and any Government of India notifications restricting transfers to specified countries

## 11. Children's Data

Our services are aimed at businesses, not individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact **privacy@zentrotech.com** and we will delete it.

Under the DPDP Act, processing children's data requires verifiable parental consent and is restricted from tracking, behavioural monitoring, and targeted advertising. We do not engage in any such activity.
