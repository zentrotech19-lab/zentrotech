1 Brand consistency

Logo, favicon, page titles, OG image — the first-impression layer.

CriticalBrowser tab title is duplicated: "Build. Automate. Get Paid. · ZentroTECH · ZentroTECH"

Why: The Next.js metadata template "%s · ZentroTECH" is appended to a homepage title that already ends with "ZentroTECH". Looks unprofessional in Chrome tab, in browser history, and in shared bookmarks.

Fix: Edit app/layout.tsx — either set the homepage's default title to just "Build. Automate. Get Paid." (the template will append), or remove the template suffix.

CriticalPhone link in footer has spaces — breaks on some Android dialers

Why: Footer renders href="tel:+91 73489 61600" with spaces. Floating CTA correctly uses tel:+917348961600. Inconsistent, and tel: with spaces won't auto-dial on older Android browsers.

Fix: In components/layout/footer.tsx use SITE.phone.replace(/\s+/g, "") in the tel href (same pattern as floating CTA).

2 Content consistency & messaging

Numbers and claims that contradict each other across the site.

Important"25+ cities" vs "138 cities" — same site, two different numbers

Why: Homepage hero says "25+ South India Cities Served". Footer says "Serving 25+ cities". But /locations shows 138 cities, and the sitemap has 5,047 URLs built around 138 cities. Sophisticated buyers will spot the inconsistency; SEO tools will flag it.

Fix: Pick one canonical claim ("138 cities" is stronger and matches reality). Update lib/constants.ts SITE.serviceAreaCount and components/sections/hero.tsx stats block + footer copy. Cascading update fixes ~6 spots.

ImportantService Areas section: "Tamil Nadu tier-2" header above mostly-Karnataka cities

Why: Homepage Service Areas tier C card is labelled "Tamil Nadu tier-2" but contains Mysore, Mangalore, Kozhikode (Karnataka + Kerala) along with Madurai. Wrong taxonomy — an attentive Bangalore SMB owner would notice.

Fix: Rename to "South India tier-2 metros" in the section labels (already used for tier D, just align tier C).
