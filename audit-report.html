✅ Critical fixes shipped (6/6)

FixedF-12Contact form silently dropped every lead

Was: app/api/contact/route.ts only console.log'd submissions with a // TODO: wire to Resend / WhatsApp comment. Every form fill returned a success screen, but no notification reached the owner.

Now: the handler validates → logs structured data → sends a branded HTML+text email via Resend (when RESEND_API_KEY env var is set) → POSTs to an optional webhook (LEAD_WEBHOOK_URL for Zapier / n8n / Slack) → returns a WhatsApp deep-link with the lead details pre-filled. Email/webhook failures are logged but never break the user-facing success.

Action needed from you: add RESEND_API_KEY (and optionally LEAD_EMAIL_FROM / LEAD_EMAIL_TO / LEAD_WEBHOOK_URL) in Vercel → Settings → Environment Variables. Until you do, leads land in server logs + the WhatsApp fallback URL.

FixedF-33No Privacy / Terms / Refund pages (DPDP Act exposure)

Was: all three returned 404. Contact form's consent checkbox cited a privacy policy that didn't exist; “money-back trial” was promised but had no policy backing it.

Now: three real pages shipped under app/(marketing)/{privacy,terms,refund}/page.tsx. Each is a proper server component with metadata, written for India's DPDP Act 2023 (grievance officer, retention, lawful basis, all 6 data-principal rights), the Mediation Act 2023 dispute path, and a clean Refund Policy that backs the money-back trial. Linked from the footer (new “Privacy · Terms · Refund” row) and added to the sitemap.

Now inserted on the homepage too. The four pages that already embedded TrustSignals automatically get the honest version.
