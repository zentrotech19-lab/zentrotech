The bottom line

Our biggest competitive risk isn't WATI, AiSensy, or LimeChat — they're enterprise SaaS platforms hunting different prey. Three smaller, faster operators are sitting in our exact lane in Bangalore + Jaipur, and two of them publish things we don't (yet): their prices. If we don't close those gaps in the next 14 days, we're the higher-friction option on every shortlist.

1

OperateAI (Ajay Singhadiya, Jaipur) publishes every price — on his homepage

Critical
Founder: Ajay Singhadiya
City: Jaipur (operates remote)
Stack: Meta API + Bolna/Sarvam + Razorpay + n8n
Threat level: 5/5

What they actually do

OperateAI runs an indie one-man AI automation agency aimed at exactly our ICP — Indian SMBs that want WhatsApp + voice + payment-recovery as a done-for-you service. The differentiator is radical transparency: setup ₹45,000–₹1,20,000 one-time + maintenance ₹8,000–₹12,000/month, full tech stack named, real case studies with concrete numbers (e.g. "₹1,500/mo running cost, 200–300 messages/day, 96% accuracy"). Ajay posts founder-bylined builds on LinkedIn and was profiled in a SiliconIndia piece on solo founders using AI tools.

Strategic implication for ZentroTECH

Hidden pricing is a 2018 tactic. Every Bangalore SMB evaluating us is now comparing against Ajay's published price card. Our /contact form asks them to talk to us; his homepage tells them the number. He wins the trust race in the first 30 seconds of the buyer journey.

Counter-move

Ship a /pricing page on zentrotech.in within 14 days. Publish three tiers (Starter, Growth, Mid-market) with concrete ranges — see the pricing landscape table below. Also publish our stack (Next.js + Meta WhatsApp API + Bolna/Sarvam voice + Razorpay + n8n) so prospects can verify we're not a black-box reseller. This single move neutralizes Ajay's edge.

2

Jinconnect Bangalore — ₹19,999/mo + a 100% refund guarantee already on their site

Critical
City: Bangalore
Public face: Imran Khan (Digital Marketing Manager)
Pricing model: Tiered retainer
Threat level: 5/5

What they actually do

Jinconnect is a Bangalore mid-tier agency publishing two retainer tiers on its homepage: "Growth Starter" at ₹19,999/month and "Scale & Dominate" at ₹39,999/month. They lead the homepage with the headline "No results, 100% refund" — identical to our money-back trial — and they got there first. Their LinkedIn company page is active, their pricing is anchored low against the field, and they've effectively pre-empted our guarantee as a differentiator.

Strategic implication for ZentroTECH

Our money-back trial is not unique anymore. A Bangalore SMB comparing Jinconnect's ₹19,999/mo refund-guaranteed retainer to our "talk to us for a quote" page picks Jinconnect every time. We need to differentiate on what we ship in 30 days, not just on the guarantee. The infrastructure proof — "5,050 SEO pages indexed in 14 days" — matters more now than the refund promise.

Counter-move

Three plays, in order: (a) Position our Starter tier below Jinconnect's ₹19,999 (we recommend ₹9,999–₹14,999/mo entry, see table) but include voice + payment recovery they don't bundle. (b) Anchor every sales conversation on what arrives in week 2 of the engagement — a fully indexed lead-engine site, not a dashboard login. (c) Reframe the guarantee from "money-back" to "first-result-in-21-days or money-back" — specific, outcome-anchored, harder to copy.

3

DigiOn (Amit Kumar, Bangalore) ships the same exact stack — but gates everything behind "Talk to CEO"

Important
Founder: Amit Kumar
City: Bangalore
Stack overlap: Web + WhatsApp + AI voice + automation
Threat level: 4/5

What they actually do

DigiOn is the closest service-stack match to ZentroTECH in Bangalore: a single agency selling websites, WhatsApp chatbots, AI voice, and automation as one stack. They have a smart vertical play — a dedicated landing page targeting interior designers — that nobody else in Bangalore has built. Their weakness: every conversion path leads to "Talk to CEO" or "Book a Call" with no published pricing, no team page, no published case studies. The funnel friction is high.

Strategic implication for ZentroTECH

DigiOn already proved the vertical-landing-page tactic works in Bangalore. They just didn't push it across enough verticals. Their friction is our opening: speed of response and clarity of price beat their gated funnel every time a buyer is in a hurry — which is most SMB buyers.

Counter-move

Build vertical-specific landing pages for dental clinics, coaching institutes, wedding photographers, sub-broker real estate, and contractors — verticals where no competitor has a landing page today (verified in the scan). Each page: vertical-specific pain points + 3 sample flows + price range + "What you get in 21 days." Our existing 5,050-page programmatic SEO infrastructure already supports this pattern; we just need 5 vertical-content templates.
