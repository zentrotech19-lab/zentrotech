## ZentroTECH Launches in Bangalore as an Agentic-First AI Consultancy Targeting India, the UAE, and Global Markets

**New Bangalore-headquartered firm with a Dubai office aims to close the gap between slow enterprise consultancies and brittle automation boutiques, serving mid-market businesses and ambitious startups with production-grade AI.**

---

**BANGALORE, India and DUBAI, UAE — May 1, 2026** — ZentroTECH, a new agentic-first AI consultancy, today announced its public launch with offices in Bangalore (HQ) and DIFC Dubai. The company is targeting mid-market businesses and fast-moving startups across India, the United Arab Emirates, the USA, and the United Kingdom — a segment its founders describe as "structurally underserved" by both legacy enterprise consulting firms and small automation shops.

ZentroTECH offers six core services: AI Agent Development, AI Automation, AI-Powered Websites, Agentic Coding adoption, LLM Integration, and AI Strategy Consulting. Each is delivered with what the company calls a "ship-first" engagement model — clients see working code within weeks rather than waiting months for slide decks.

### The market gap

Analysis of the current AI consulting landscape, conducted by ZentroTECH ahead of launch, found a clear positioning gap. Enterprise giants such as Fractal and Mu Sigma command Fortune 500 budgets and engagement timelines that often stretch to six months before any code reaches production. At the other end of the market, hundreds of regional automation boutiques compete on price but lack the engineering depth to architect production-grade agentic systems.

"There is a significant under-served middle," said the company's founder. "Mid-market businesses in Bangalore, ambitious enterprises across the GCC, and Series A startups in London and New York all want real AI — agents that think, automation that scales, websites that convert. They do not want to spend a quarter in discovery before anyone writes code, and they do not want to hire someone whose entire toolkit is Zapier."

Competitive analysis cited in the company's positioning materials notes that established players including Fractal, Mu Sigma, GeekyAnts, and the Big Four consultancies are slow and expensive; UAE specialists such as G42 and Presight focus on government scale; regional brands such as Mrkhan Digital and JADA Squad are limited in geographic reach or service breadth. ZentroTECH says it is the only AI consultancy combining four characteristics: agentic-AI-first expertise, full-service AI consultancy breadth, an SMB-accessible engagement model, and award-worthy global design.

### Built for the next ten years of AI

The company's tagline — "Engineering the AI of 2050, today" — reflects a deliberate orientation toward the agentic era of AI rather than the older era of dashboards, predictive models, and chatbot pilots.

"We believe the next decade of business value in AI will come from autonomous agents that take real action, not from another generation of dashboards," the founder added. "We built ZentroTECH to be the consultancy we wished existed when we first tried to ship agents in production at previous companies. Real evals. Real cost monitoring. Real production handovers. No vapor."

### Footprint and team

ZentroTECH operates from WeWork Galaxy on Residency Road in Bangalore as its headquarters and from DIFC Innovation Hub in Dubai as its GCC office. The team is composed of AI engineers, full-stack developers, designers, and strategy consultants. The company is actively hiring across both locations.

Initial client engagements span retail, logistics, financial services, SaaS, and government services across four geographies. The company plans to publish case studies on a rolling basis as engagements conclude.

### Public availability

The full ZentroTECH website, including its AI Agent Showcase with interactive demos, six service deep-dive pages, and city-specific landing pages for Bangalore, Dubai, and the wider UAE, is now live at https://zentrotech.ai. Discovery calls can be booked at https://zentrotech.ai/contact.
