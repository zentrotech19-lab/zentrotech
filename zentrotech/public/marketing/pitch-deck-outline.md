# ZentroTECH Pitch Deck — 12 Slide Outline

**Use cases:** Enterprise client pitch (primary). Investor pitch (secondary, with the noted ASK slide swap). Conference talk (use slides 1-6 only).
**Format suggestion:** 16:9, 1920x1080. Dark theme. Aurora gradient accents. Geist Bold for headlines, Inter Regular for body, JetBrains Mono for stats.
**Total speaking time:** 15 minutes. ~75 seconds per slide.
**Visual style:** Match the website. Glassmorphism cards on slides showing product. Full-bleed gradient backgrounds on transition slides (1, 5, 11). Whitespace-heavy on data slides (8, 10).
