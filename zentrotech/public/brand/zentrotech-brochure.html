01 / Who we are
Introduction

An agentic-first AI consultancy, purpose-built for the 2050 economy.

We are engineers, designers, and operators who ship production AI for a living. Founded in 2026 in Bangalore with a presence in Dubai, ZentroTECH was built around one conviction: agentic AI is the new application layer — and the businesses that adopt it well will compound for a decade.

We sit in a deliberate gap. The tier-one strategy houses are slow and slide-deck-heavy. The boutique automation shops are narrow. The AI labs are not consultancies. We combine senior agentic engineering with full-service AI breadth, priced so that ambitious mid-market and SMB teams can actually engage us — without giving up the premium delivery global enterprises expect.

Every engagement is run by a small, senior team that ships in vertical slices. Weekly demos, written evaluations on every change, human-in-the-loop guardrails, and a fixed-price proposal after a paid 1–2 week discovery sprint. You see a working v1 in the first month, or you stop. No long sales cycles, no slide-deck dance.

We work with funded startups in Bangalore, mid-market enterprises across India and the UAE, and tech-forward teams in the US, UK, and Singapore. About half our pipeline is international — we run async-first with overlapping working hours and travel for kickoffs and quarterly reviews when it earns its keep.

03 / How we work
A four-step engagement

From discovery to defence — production AI in weeks, not quarters.

Every ZentroTECH engagement runs the same disciplined loop. Paid discovery up front so pricing is honest. Vertical slices so you see value early and can stop after any one. Written evaluations so we both know whether the system works. Ongoing care so it keeps working as the world changes.

STEP 01
Discover

Map the problem, prove the feasibility, fix the price.

A paid 1–2 week sprint where we interview stakeholders, audit data, prototype the riskiest part, and write a fixed-price proposal. By the end you have a yes/no on feasibility and a quote you can sign — not a slide deck.

DELIVERABLES: opportunity map · technical spike · SOW · eval plan
STEP 02
Design

Architect the system, write the eval, plan the slices.

We design the agent topology, tool integrations, memory and retrieval layers, guardrails, and observability. We also write the evaluation harness that scores every change to come — before we write production code.

DELIVERABLES: system architecture · eval harness v1 · UX flows · slice roadmap
STEP 03
Deploy

Ship vertical slices weekly until v1 is in production.

Each slice is a working, testable, demoable increment of the final system. Weekly demos, weekly evals, weekly cost and quality reports. v1 in production in 4–12 weeks for most engagements; you can stop after any slice.

DELIVERABLES: production system · IaC · runbooks · handover docs
STEP 04
Defend & Grow

Monitor, regression-test, and compound the wins.

Optional monthly retainer covering monitoring, eval regression on every model change, prompt and cost tuning, and incremental capability additions. Most clients keep us on for 6–12 months post-launch.

DELIVERABLES: monthly review · eval reports · model upgrades · new capabilities
