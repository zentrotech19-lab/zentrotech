## 4. What format works in this niche

Cross-referenced from search index + niche samples:

- **Single tweet > thread for B2B India.** Indian SaaS founders' highest-engagement posts are punchy single tweets or screenshots of LinkedIn carousels reposted as image tweets. Threads underperform unless they're build-in-public numbers.
- **Screenshots of real chats / dashboards / WhatsApp conversations** outperform stock graphics 3–5x.
- **₹/INR numbers in the first line** — Indian audience filters for INR signals. "We saved a Bangalore furniture brand ₹47K/mo on WhatsApp marketing in 3 weeks" beats "we reduced costs significantly".
- **Best posting time (B2B India):** Tue–Thu, **9–11am IST and 1–3pm IST**. Avoid Mon mornings (catch-up) and Fri afternoons.
- **Hashtags that aren't spammy:** `#BuildInPublic`, `#IndianSaaS`, `#WhatsAppBusiness`, `#BharatAI`. Avoid `#DigitalIndia`, `#GrowthHacking`, `#StartupIndia` — flooded with bots.
- **The format that's working in our space right now:** Operator post-mortems. "We tried X with a client, here's the number we got, here's what we'd do differently." Dharmesh Ba's thread is a textbook example.

## Operating notes for @ZentroTECH19

- **Post 5 days/week minimum, Tue–Sat.** Skip Sunday and Monday morning.
- **Two windows: 9:30–11am IST, 1–3pm IST.** Reply window: 8pm–10pm IST (when Indian operators wind down and scroll).
- **Ratio:** 60% reply/quote-tweet, 30% original posts, 10% promotional. Never pin a promotional tweet.
- **Pin tweet should be a result, not a manifesto.** A real client number with the anonymised dashboard.
- **Never use AI-image graphics.** Always real screenshots, real call-recording waveforms, real WhatsApp threads (with names blurred).
- **No emojis in the first line.** They're a tell that an account is bot-managed.
- **Founder voice ≠ brand voice.** When the ZentroTECH founder gets an X handle, the founder replies to threads, the brand handle posts results. Don't mix.
