**Day 25 | Thu 19 Jun | Behind-the-scenes | 6:00 PM IST**
*Hook:*
`Spent today rebuilding our voice agent's interruption-handling. The fix took 4 lines.`
*Body:*
Old behaviour: agent finishes its sentence even when the caller interrupts. Felt robotic.

New behaviour: 250ms VAD threshold + immediate yield to the caller. Resume only when the caller pauses >700ms.

The 4 lines that mattered:
```
const VAD_INTERRUPT_MS = 250;
const RESUME_AFTER_MS = 700;
on(speech.start, () => agent.yield());
on(speech.end(RESUME_AFTER_MS), () => agent.resume());
```

Tested across 30 calls in Kannada, Hindi, Tamil. Caller satisfaction (post-call NPS) went from 6.4 to 8.1.

The unsexy details are what makes voice agents not feel like voice agents.

## Section 4 — Daily engagement workflow (30 min/day, repeatable)

### Morning block — 15 min (8:00-8:15 AM IST)
1. **(3 min)** Open LinkedIn -> publish the day's scheduled post (use Buffer/Hypefury for queueing the night before — the morning step is just "review + ship").
2. **(2 min)** Re-read first comment slot; pin a primer comment with extra detail or a question to bait replies.
3. **(10 min)** Engage with 3 specific accounts' newest posts. Rotate from a list of 30 priority targets so each gets engagement ~once per 10 days. Use these comment templates (never "great post"):
   - **Add a data point**: "We see similar — our pilot data on 14 Blr SMBs shows X. Maps to your point about Y."
   - **Disagree carefully**: "Agree on A. On B, we've actually seen the opposite in 6 D2C pilots — possibly because [specific reason]."
   - **Ask a sharp Q**: "Curious — does this still hold for sub-Rs.5Cr SMBs, or does it break below a certain rev band?"

### Afternoon block — 15 min (1:30-1:45 PM IST)
4. **(5 min)** Respond to every comment on your own post. Thank the bots only with a heart-react. Reply with a follow-up Q to humans.
5. **(7 min)** Send the day's 10 connection requests from the Section-3 list. Personal 50-char note attached to every single one. Never blank.
6. **(3 min)** React (not just like — choose Insightful / Celebrate where honest) to 20 posts from your feed. LinkedIn weighs your engagement on others into your post distribution.

### Evening block — under 5 min (10:00 PM IST)
7. Log into Section 5 tracker: impressions, reactions, comments, profile visits, DMs.
8. Save anything that worked (screenshot the post + numbers into `/zentro/linkedin-wins/`).
9. Tomorrow's post: open the draft, eyeball it, queue for 8 AM.

## Section 7 — Red lines & don'ts

1. **Don't post 4 times a day.** LinkedIn throttles same-account multi-post reach. Once daily, max.
2. **Don't tag people without context.** Only tag if (a) they're literally referenced in the post or (b) the post adds value to their narrative. Never tag for "amplification" alone — it nukes trust and they'll mute you.
3. **Don't post generic AI graphics.** No Midjourney "futuristic Indian SMB" stock. Use real screenshots — GSC, WhatsApp Business Manager, Razorpay dashboard, our own code. Anonymise client names with a black bar.
4. **Don't hashtag spam.** Max 5. India-relevant only. Never `#DigitalIndia #StartupIndia #InnovationDriven` — they signal low effort.
5. **Don't auto-comment or use comment-bots.** Will get flagged + tanks reach.
6. **Don't trash competitors by name.** Reference categories ("most BSPs", "the dashboard-first crowd"). Never call out AiSensy / WATI / Interakt by name negatively — you will lose any chance of being reposted by their networks.
7. **Don't post case studies without permission.** Anonymise vertical + city + numbers until you have written go-ahead from the client. One ego-driven name-drop kills a partnership.
8. **Don't connect with cold notes that ask for time in line 1.** First 5 connects with someone = comment, react, comment, DM-no-ask, then ask.
9. **Don't post on Sundays before 4 PM** (Indian audience is offline; impressions cap at 30% of weekday).
10. **Don't run paid amplification in the first 30 days.** Burn the warmup organically — paid distorts your learning loop and you won't know what's actually working.
11. **Don't recycle one post on two profiles** (founder profile + brand profile) on the same day. LinkedIn dedupes and tanks both.
12. **Don't claim numbers we don't have yet.** Placeholders in this doc stay placeholders until pilots produce real receipts. "30-50% DSO drop" is our category claim; "we cut clinic X's DSO 40%" needs to actually be true.
13. **Don't use the founder's email anywhere in posts.** Direct everyone to DM or zentrotech.in.
14. **Don't post our 3D mascot or video work in production-facing posts** until quality bar is met (per existing project rule). Stills only, and only with explicit OK.
