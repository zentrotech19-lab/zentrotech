🚫One channel we will NOT run brand-suicide for us

❌ Cold WhatsApp outbound

Reply rate is shockingly high (10–20% on the first 50 manual sends) — but the number gets banned by message ~200 for unsolicited B2B messaging. We sell WhatsApp compliance as a service. Getting our own number banned is brand-suicide. Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy + DPDP Act make this a non-starter regardless of how tempting the early numbers look. Do not run.
